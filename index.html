About

This page is intended to showcase my projects. My projects are mostly just things I find cool and have no guarantee to be useful to anyone or anything. I hope someone enjoys them as much as I do. I am interested in automation, image processing, and proof-of-concept projects.

Background

My name is Jake, and I am fascinated by technology. I became interested in the field of Computer Science as a senior in high school. One requirement for my pre-calculus class was a Ti-84 calculator. During the class, someone showed me a quadratic formula program, and I was amazed. Just a few lines of code could vastly improve and simplify my life. I went on to create dozens of programs in Ti-Basic, and my addiction was formed. I have been learning and coding ever since.
